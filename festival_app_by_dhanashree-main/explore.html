January – Makar Sankranti

Kites fly high and sweets are shared to mark the harvest season in Maharashtra.

February – Shiv Jayanti

Honoring Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj with pride and patriotic celebrations.

March – Holi

Festival of colors and unity with vibrant powders and joyful gatherings.

April – Gudhi Padwa

Maharashtrian New Year marked with Gudhi flags and traditional meals.

May – Akshaya Tritiya

One of the most auspicious days for beginnings and investments.

June – Vat Purnima

Married women tie threads around banyan trees praying for their husbands' long lives.

July – Ashadi Ekadashi

Mass pilgrimage to Pandharpur in devotion to Lord Vitthal.

August – Raksha Bandhan

Bond of love between brothers and sisters celebrated with rakhi and gifts.

September – Ganesh Chaturthi

Maharashtra’s biggest festival, celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha.

October – Navratri

Nine nights of worship, fasting, dancing, and devotion to Goddess Durga.

November – Diwali

Festival of Lights celebrated with lamps, sweets, and fireworks.

December – Datta Jayanti

Birth anniversary of Lord Dattatreya with spiritual celebrations.
